Fire Prevention - After knowning what causes fire, we should know learn how to prevent them.

Cooking
  • Remember NOT to leave cooking food unattended
  • Keep area free from any Flamable or Combustible Materials.
  • Always keep cooking equidment clean.
  • Keep a fire extinguisher nearby (if you somehow do have one).
    		•

    Electrical Equipment
  • Always keep appliances that produce heat unplugged when unused.
  • Use extension cords temporarily only.
  • Keep electrical cords away from a liquid material.
    		•

    Smoking
  • NEVER smoke while inside a house
  • Avoid smoke inside a house after drinking alcohol.
  • Always smoke outside.
  • Put cigarettes fully out.
    		•

    Chemicals and Gasses
  • Keep any substance away from any heat.
  • Keep containers closed.
